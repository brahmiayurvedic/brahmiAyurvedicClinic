About Us

Brahmi Ayurvedic Clinic

Established in 2023 by Dr. Meghana B, the Brahmi Ayurvedic Clinic embodies holistic well-being.

With a vision to provide comprehensive care, Dr. Meghana has crafted a space where ancient Ayurvedic principles are used to guide patients on their journey towards natural healing.

Their motto, "Wellness is a journey, not a destination," reflects their dedication to helping people achieve lasting health. By providing personal consultations and traditional remedies, the clinic promotes balance in life, tending not only to physical health but also nurturing mental and emotional peace.

Dr. Meghana B

In Dr. Meghana's words, "At the beginning, ayurveda was a course of study for me. Over the course of time, I closely observed that patients who were taking ayurvedic medications and treatments saw continuous improvements in their health. At this point, I came to the realization that Ayurveda is a way of life. My passion is to guide you on your journey towards natural healing, offering customised ayurvedic consultations, and embracing a healthy life based on principles of Ayurveda."

Dr. Meghana has completed her Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery at Sri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwara (SDM) College of Ayurveda & Hospital, Bengaluru where she secured the following ranks:

  • 9th rank in first professional year
  • 3rd rank in Kriya Shareera (human physiology ) in Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences
